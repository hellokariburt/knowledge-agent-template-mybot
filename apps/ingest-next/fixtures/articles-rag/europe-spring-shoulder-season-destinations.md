8 European destinations that are better in spring than summer

By traveling during Europe's spring shoulder season, you can beat the high-season crowds, skip the heat waves and get a great deal along the way.

Spring shoulder season in Europe (March to May) offers milder weather, fewer crowds and better value compared to peak summer months. Destinations like Paris, Seville, Amsterdam, Venice, the Algarve, Ireland, Crete and Dubrovnik are highlighted for their unique springtime experiences, from cherry blossoms to local festivals and outdoor activities. Travelers can enjoy easier access to popular attractions, seasonal events like tulip viewing and regional cuisine such as Portugal's sardine season. Hotel stays using loyalty points are featured, with several properties offering memorable accommodations and perks during this period. Spring shoulder season avoids the extreme heat and dense crowds typical of summer, but some destinations may still see increased activity during special events or festivals. Certain attractions and gardens reopen in spring, so check opening dates and event calendars when planning. Loyalty program point values for hotel stays vary by property and destination, with rates and availability subject to change.
