Hilton Honors American Express Card: Solid perks for beginner travelers

These are the pros and cons of the Hilton Honors Amex Card, as well as competing cards that might merit your attention.

An entry-level hotel rewards card provides automatic Silver elite status and solid earning at Hilton locations, with no annual fee required. Cardholders can access exclusive event presales and receive travel and purchase protections, including extended warranty and purchase protection. The card is best for those who stay at Hilton properties occasionally and want to build points without premium perks or fees. Higher status and additional benefits are available on other products, but require higher spending or a fee. Gold status is only earned after $20,000 in spending within a calendar year; other products offer this automatically. Best value is achieved when redeeming points for hotel stays, especially with the fifth-night-free benefit for award bookings. Points are valued at 0.5 cents each as of February 2026 per TPG's valuations.
