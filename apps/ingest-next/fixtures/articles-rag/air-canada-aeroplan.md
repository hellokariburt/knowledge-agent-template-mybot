Air Canada’s Aeroplan program: The ultimate guide to earning and redeeming points

Everything you need to know about Aeroplan, Air Canada's loyalty program, including how to earn elite status and the best ways to earn and redeem points.

Air Canada's Aeroplan program is a leading Star Alliance loyalty option, offering published award charts for most partner flights and low fees on award tickets. Aeroplan points can be earned easily through various transferable points programs and retail partners, making it accessible even for U.S.-based travelers. The program features flexible redemption options, including the ability to add stopovers to one-way tickets for a modest points surcharge, and does not impose fuel surcharges on awards. Aeroplan elite status provides tiered benefits such as priority services, lounge access, and reciprocal perks with Star Alliance partners. Some partner airlines only offer limited earning or redemption opportunities, with certain partnerships restricted to codeshare routes or select flights. Award pricing for Air Canada and select partners uses dynamic pricing, so costs may vary significantly by date and route. A partner booking fee applies to some award tickets, and stopovers cannot be added within North America.
