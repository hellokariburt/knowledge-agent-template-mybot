Monthly checklist: Credit card perks and benefits you should be using in March

There are numerous monthly perks and credits you should be using on your credit cards in March 2026. We've compiled them here so you won't forget about them.

Many credit cards offer monthly or quarterly statement credits for categories like dining, streaming, ride-hailing and travel bookings. Some benefits are "use it or lose it," expiring at the end of each month or quarter if not redeemed. Enrollment or activation is often required to access these perks, and credits may not roll over to future periods. A monthly checklist can help cardholders track and maximize these time-sensitive benefits. Credits typically require advance enrollment or activation and may only apply to specific merchants or services. Unused credits generally expire at the end of each period and cannot be carried over. Some benefits are split into semiannual or quarterly amounts, so timing your redemptions is important.
