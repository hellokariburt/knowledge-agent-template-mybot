Ice camping, kayaking, snowshoeing: The epic adventure of an HX Expeditions trip to Antarctica

What's it like on a HX Expeditions trip to Antarctica? TPG's Gene Sloan offers a firsthand look.

A 16-night Antarctic expedition aboard a modern hybrid-powered ship offers immersive experiences like camping, kayaking and snowshoeing in remote, wildlife-rich settings. The vessel stands out for its advanced equipment, efficient Zodiac deployment and a wide range of adventure gear, enabling more activities than many similar ships. Comfortable accommodations, quality dining and wellness amenities provide a midmarket experience, with pricing notably lower than luxury competitors. Sustainability is a core focus, with hybrid-electric technology, reduced emissions and support for scientific research, including opportunities for passenger involvement. Optional activities such as kayaking, camping and snowshoeing incur extra charges and are subject to weather conditions. The ship carries more passengers than most Antarctic expedition vessels, resulting in a less intimate atmosphere but greater affordability. Announcements are multilingual, reflecting a diverse passenger mix and potentially longer briefings.
