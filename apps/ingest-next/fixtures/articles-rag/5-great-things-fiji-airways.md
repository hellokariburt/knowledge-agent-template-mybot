5 things I loved about flying Fiji Airways business class

Fiji Airways business class impressed with warm Fijian hospitality, dine-on-demand meals and decent award availability.

Fiji Airways offers strong business-class award availability between North America and Fiji, with onward connections to Australia and New Zealand. The airline's Airbus A350 features high-quality service, restaurant-style dining and distinctive Fijian touches that create a relaxing onboard experience. Award seats can often be booked at saver rates through several airline loyalty programs, sometimes with easier access than larger carriers on similar routes. A stopover in Fiji provides a peaceful and hospitable break, with the island's welcoming culture and scenic beaches enhancing the overall journey. Award availability may fluctuate, with additional seats sometimes released closer to departure, so setting alerts can be helpful. Booking through certain loyalty programs can offer lower redemption rates, but saver-level space is required for the best value. Transfers from select transferable points programs are possible, but terms and eligibility may vary.
