How good is the Atmos Ascent's 70,000-point bonus and its $99 Companion Fare?

We break down the value of the Atmos Ascent card’s 70,000-point bonus and $99 Companion Fare to see if this offer is truly worth it for flyers.

A new airline rewards card offers a substantial bonus of points and a companion fare after meeting an initial spend requirement. The current bonus is notable for its high value and relatively low spending threshold compared to previous offers. Points can be redeemed for valuable flights with Alaska Airlines, Hawaiian Airlines and Oneworld partners, including premium cabins and short-haul routes. Additional benefits include a companion fare each year after meeting an annual spend, discounts on lounge membership and travel perks such as free checked bags and preferred boarding. Eligibility for the bonus is subject to specific application restrictions, including limits based on recent card openings across all issuers. Points and companion fare are valued at $1,149 combined, per TPG's February 2026 valuations. The card does not provide complimentary lounge access or travel protections such as baggage delay or trip cancellation coverage.
