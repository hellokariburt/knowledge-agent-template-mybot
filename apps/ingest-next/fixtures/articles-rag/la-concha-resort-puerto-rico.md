Beach views and comfy rooms: A review of La Concha Resort, Puerto Rico, Autograph Collection

The gorgeous ocean views, mouthwatering meals and spacious, comfortable accommodations made it hard to leave La Concha Resort, Puerto Rico, Autograph Collection.

La Concha Resort in San Juan, Puerto Rico, offers a beachfront location in the lively Condado neighborhood, featuring modern design, renovated rooms and a vibrant atmosphere. The property boasts three pools, a full-service spa, multiple dining options and a casino, appealing to travelers seeking both relaxation and entertainment. Recent renovations have added redesigned suites, new dining concepts and expanded wellness amenities, enhancing the guest experience. Guests can book stays using a major hotel loyalty program or pay flexible cash rates, though a nightly resort fee applies. Award nights are dynamically priced, with some room types—including suites—available for the same minimum point redemption on select dates. A nightly resort fee includes access to amenities like bike rentals, fitness classes, beach equipment and casino credits. Beach conditions at Condado can be challenging for swimmers; nearby Escambron Beach offers calmer waters.
