Bilt's best-ever transfer bonus? Book a business-class flight to Japan starting at 25K points

Bilt Rewards is arguably offering its best-ever transfer bonus to Japan Airlines Mileage Bank. Here's how you can book a business-class flight to Japan for 25,000 points.

A special transfer bonus is available for one day only on March 1, allowing Bilt Rewards members to convert points to Japan Airlines Mileage Bank miles with bonuses ranging from 25% to 125%, depending on status and Bilt Cash redemption. Members can use $135 in Bilt Cash to unlock a higher transfer bonus, with Platinum status holders able to reach the maximum 125% bonus. Japan Airlines miles are valuable for booking flights to Japan and Oneworld partner flights, with award rates starting at 27,000 miles for economy and 55,000 miles for business class from North America. Award seat availability is limited, especially at saver levels, but Japan Airlines opens bookings 360 days in advance, offering a head start over other programs. Japan Airlines Mileage Bank accounts must be at least 60 days old to redeem miles, though Bilt members with newer accounts face a seven-day hold after transfer. Miles expire after three years with no extension options, and transfers cannot be reversed. Japan Airlines miles can only be used for you and direct family members, not friends or unrelated companions.
