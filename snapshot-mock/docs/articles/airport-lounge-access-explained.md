Lounge access can come from card memberships, airline status, or premium cabin tickets. For occasional travelers, card-based access is usually the most predictable.

## What matters most

- Guest policy (how many people are included)
- Network coverage at your home airport
- Visit caps and change dates

## Common mistakes

- Assuming all lounges are included globally
- Ignoring card-specific enrollment steps
- Forgetting that authorized users may have different access terms

## Recommendation pattern

Choose based on your actual airport pattern first, then compare annual fee offsets from lounge value and travel credits.
